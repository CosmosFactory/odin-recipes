Chicken fillet

Description

Chicken fillets are a cut of chicken usually taken from the breast region of the bird. They are boneless pieces of meat that are mostly flat, although not usually of a consistent thickness through the length of the meat.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Chicken Breast
  • Cornstarch
  • Egg
  • Fish sauce
  • Garlic
  • Black Pepper Ground
  • Oil
