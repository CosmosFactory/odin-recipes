Chicken fillet

Description

Chicken fillets are a cut of chicken usually taken from the breast region of the bird. They are boneless pieces of meat that are mostly flat, although not usually of a consistent thickness through the length of the meat.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Chicken Breast
  • Cornstarch
  • Egg
  • Fish sauce
  • Garlic
  • Black Pepper Ground
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Cut chicken into halves lengthwise.
  2. Combine water and salt in a large bowl. Then add the chicken fillets and let them sit in the saltwater to brine for at least 15 minutes.
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk together the egg, oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  4. In another bowl, add breadcrumbs, paprika, onion powder, and parmesan cheese.
  5. Add chicken fillets into the egg mixture
  6. Then dredge the chicken in the breadcrumb mixture.
